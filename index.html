The Silver Road
to Thamel

For generations, the Tamrakar family were seekers of material. Our ancestors traveled into Tibet to procure the gold and silver that would eventually be hammered into the sacred ornaments of Kathmandu’s temples.

Today, my workshop in Thamel carries on this "slow silver" movement. We do not believe in mass production. We believe a piece should take as long as it needs to be perfect.

"Silver is a living material, it remembers the strike of the hammer."

Prabin Tamrakar

Proprietor & Master Smith
